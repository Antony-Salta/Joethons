Home to over 8 billion people, Earth is our home planet! Earth is the only planet in the solar system we can currently live on, mainly due to air. Humans need air to breathe, and Earth is the only planet with Oxygen for us to breathe. Earth sits between Venus and Mars in the solar system, and only has one moon (The Moon). Earth has a gravity of 9.8 m/s^2, which means when you jump you don't just fly away!
